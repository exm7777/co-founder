# The charter template

The charter is the one file every skill reads before working. It is advisory context loaded in
full every session, so every line has to earn its place: before writing any line, ask "would
removing this cause a mistake?" If not, it stays out. Target under 150 lines at generation so
the file has headroom to grow before it needs a prune.

Section order is fixed: it matches how the model scans. Placeholders are marked `<like this>`;
fill every one with the founder's words from the interview, or delete the line if they skipped it.

---

```markdown
# <Business name>: <founder first name>'s business brain

<!-- Generated by /co-founder:co-founder-setup on <date>. This file is AGENTS.md; CLAUDE.md links to it.
     Edit through the skills, or ask directly. -->

<2-4 lines, concrete: what the business sells, to whom, at what stage. "A 6-week pricing course
for freelance designers, ~$3k/mo, growing the email list toward a cohort launch", never
"a coaching business".>

## Laws

1. **Gate law.** Nothing gets built until it has been scoped through the readiness gates and
   signed off. Execution refuses ungated work. Autonomy is earned stage by stage. YOU MUST NOT
   skip this.
2. **Receipts law.** Consequential advice (go/no-go, strategy, pricing, customer-facing,
   money, hard-to-reverse) needs one internal business receipt and one checked external source.
   A missing leg parks the recommendation and names recall or research as the next check.
3. **Challenge law.** A "just build it" handed over without a who-it's-for and a why gets pushed
   back on like a co-founder would, not executed silently.
4. **Always-ask law.** Anything irreversible, customer-facing, or that costs money stops and asks
   first. Reversible and cheap work still takes the light plan path: sized small, gauntlet waived on
   record, scope confirmed, then done without ceremony.
5. **<Founder law from interview Q5, e.g. "Never discount below $400.">**
6. **<Founder law, e.g. "No cold outreach. Inbound only.">**

## Where things live

- Vision / strategy: `wiki/business/vision.md`. Read before any big call.
- Current offer: `wiki/business/offer.md` · Dated numbers: `wiki/business/metrics.md`
- Content drafts: `wiki/business/content/` · Reviews: `wiki/business/reviews/`
- The vault (learnings, SOPs, research, decisions, people): `wiki/`
- Voice file (how everything public sounds): `wiki/business/voice.md`
- Decision log (hard-to-reverse calls, dated): `wiki/decisions/`
- The catalog: `index.md` · The history: `log.md`
- Cross-skill queue: `queue.md` · Processed-source ledger: `inbox-ledger.md`
- Current work: the active initiative in `wiki/initiatives/` (state named in its frontmatter).

## When you say X, I run Y

| You say...                                              | I run            |
|---------------------------------------------------------|------------------|
| "is this good", "should we build/change price"          | `gauntlet`       |
| "what's the plan", "how should we build this"           | `plan`           |
| "just build it", "go build X", any raw execution ask     | `plan` (the gate)|
| "where are we", "what should I do today"                 | `sprint`         |
| "look this up", "get the real numbers on..."             | `research`       |
| "what do we know about...", "have we tried this"         | `recall`         |
| "what did we learn", "debrief this call"                 | `bank`           |
| "file this", "clean up the vault"                        | `steward`         |
| "how's the week", "score me"                             | `review`         |
| "design an unapproved offer/price", any unresolved money move | `gauntlet`  |
| "structure this approved offer/price"                    | `offer`          |
| "write the post", "content for this week"                | `content-engine` |
| "where is this going", "set the direction"               | `vision`         |
| not sure which                                           | `help`           |

## Rituals

- **Session open:** sprint reads the last baton note and states where things stand. Five
  lines and ONE next move.
- **Weekly review:** scorecard against the vision file. At most ONE change to how you work per
  week, defended.
- **Before any big call:** recall checks the vault first. The answer cites files, or says
  plainly that nothing is on record and offers to research.

## Autonomy

Execution refuses ungated work; trust is earned stage by stage. Unattended runs leave unknowns
open instead of guessing.
Open questions land in a "Needs your call" list instead.
```

## Fill rules

- Founder laws come verbatim from interview Q5, tightened for precision, never softened.
- The identity block uses their numbers only at the resolution they gave (a range stays a range).
- A derived number appears only after its explicit currency/unit/period formula recomputes from
  confirmed inputs. Conflicting totals stay out until the founder resolves the mismatch.
- The charter carries the founder's own words. Do not annotate its lines with provenance tags,
  and never rewrite a founder law or phrase to satisfy a validator; a personal law
  ("Never discount below $400") is kept verbatim, not reworded and not tagged as evidence.
- The glossary starts with at most the 2-3 terms the interview itself surfaced. Empty is fine.
- The trigger map ships complete as printed above: it is the collection's always-loaded router.
  When a skill is added or renamed later, this table and the `help` skill both get re-synced in
  the same pass. A trigger map that lists a dead skill is a router that lies.
- Write the founder-facing prose in their register where it appears (the identity block), and in
  the collection's plain, direct register everywhere else.
